# Pigeon Pose
```yaml
id: pigeon-pose
name_en: Pigeon Pose
category: stretching
equipment: []
camera: front 45°
start: Front shin folded across the floor, rear leg extended straight back, torso upright on the hands
finish: Torso folded forward over the front shin, forearms toward the floor, hips square and sinking
primary: [Glutes (stretched), Piriformis]
secondary: [Hip flexors (rear leg), Lower back]
avoid: [Hamstrings, Arms, Tilted hips]
mistakes: [Hips rotating open, Front foot tucked too deep]
notes: Front-leg glute is the glowing stretch zone
```
