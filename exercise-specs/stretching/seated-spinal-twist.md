# Seated Spinal Twist
```yaml
id: seated-spinal-twist
name_en: Seated Spinal Twist
category: stretching
equipment: []
camera: front 45°
start: Seated tall, one leg extended, other foot crossed over planted outside the knee
finish: Torso rotated toward the bent knee, opposite elbow braced against it, spine tall
primary: [Spinal rotators (stretched), Obliques (stretched), Glutes (stretched)]
secondary: [Lower back, Neck]
avoid: [Slumping, Forcing the twist with the arms]
mistakes: [Rounding the spine, Lifting the hips]
notes: Rotation happens along a tall vertical spine
```
