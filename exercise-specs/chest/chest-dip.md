# Chest Dip
```yaml
id: chest-dip
name_en: Chest Dip
category: chest
equipment: [Parallel dip bars]
grip: neutral on bars
camera: front 45°
start: Supported on bars, elbows bent deep, torso leaning forward ~30°, chest stretched
finish: Arms extended, body raised, forward lean maintained
primary: [Pectoralis major (lower fibers)]
secondary: [Triceps, Front deltoids]
avoid: [Lats, Biceps, Traps]
mistakes: [Upright torso — that makes it a triceps dip, Highlighting biceps]
notes: Forward lean is the defining difference from triceps dip
```
